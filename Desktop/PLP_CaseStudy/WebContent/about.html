Services Offered

Life Insurance :Life insurance is a contract between an insurer and a policyholder in which the insurer guarantees payment of a death benefit to named beneficiaries upon the death of the insured. I-NSURED promises a death benefit in consideration of the payment of premium by the insured. This is often calculated with a free asset ratio. Medical Insurance : Health insurance is a type of insurance coverage that pays for medical, surgical, and sometimes dental expenses incurred by the insured. Health insurance can reimburse the insured for expenses incurred from illness or injury, or pay the care provider directly. I-NSURED provides various plans for all kind of people with low premium amounts. House Insurance :House insurance is a form of property insurance that covers losses and damages to an individual's residence, along with furnishings and other assets in the home.I-NSURED provides liability coverage against accidents in the home or on the property. Vehicle Insurance : Vehicle/Auto insurance is a policy purchased by vehicle owners to mitigate costs associated with getting into an auto accident. Instead of paying out of pocket for auto accidents, people pay annual premiums to I-NSURED; the company then pays all or most of the costs associated with an auto accident or other vehicle damage.
